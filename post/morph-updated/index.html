May 30, 2016

Reversing the animation

We’ve now completed the goals listed in our previous blog post. The animation is now reversible and can transition to arbitrary locations on the screen. The icon of the floating action button can now also be animated along with the view.

Note the arc of the path. This effect is achieved by translating the view along a quadradic bezier path which is calculated based on the positions of the two views relative to one-another. The floating action button is also animated along this path. This allows us to fade out the icon naturally between the two views, resulting in a more convincing transition.
